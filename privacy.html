Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

The Campaign Analytics Platform collects the following data from authorized TikTok accounts via the TikTok API:

  • Public engagement metrics (views, likes, comments, shares, saves)
  • Audience demographic data (age ranges, gender distribution, geographic location)
  • Content performance data (watch time, retention rates, traffic sources)
  • Account-level analytics (follower counts, growth metrics)

2. How We Use Information

Data is used solely for internal campaign performance tracking, providing analytics dashboards to authorized clients, and optimizing content strategy across managed creator accounts.

3. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized platform administrators and relevant campaign clients. We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect stored data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share collected data with unauthorized third parties. Data may be shared in aggregated, anonymized report form with campaign clients for whom the content was created.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with TikTok's API in compliance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and data access policies.

6. Data Retention

Campaign data is retained for the duration of active campaigns and a reasonable period thereafter for reporting purposes. Data may be deleted upon request.

7. User Rights

Account owners whose data is accessed through the Service may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their data by contacting the platform administrator.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above.

9. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the platform administrator.
